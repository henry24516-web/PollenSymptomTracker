**Pollen Tracker - Take Control of Your Allergies**

Track pollen levels, log symptoms, and understand your allergy patterns with Pollen Tracker. This app combines real-time pollen data with personalized symptom logging to help you manage seasonal allergies effectively.

**Features:**

• **Real-Time Pollen Data** - Get accurate pollen forecasts for your location using data from Open-Meteo's authoritative weather and air quality APIs. Track tree, grass, and weed pollen levels.

• **Smart Symptom Logging** - Log your daily symptoms with severity levels. Track runny nose, sneezing, itchy eyes, congestion, headaches, fatigue, sore throat, and coughing.

• **Trend Analysis** - View your symptom patterns over 7, 14, or 30 days. Correlate your symptoms with pollen levels to identify triggers.

• **High Pollen Alerts** - Get notified when pollen levels are high in your area so you can take preventive measures.

• **Export Your Data** - Export your symptom logs to CSV for sharing with healthcare providers or for your own records.

• **Premium Features** - Unlock unlimited symptom logging, extended trend charts, data export, and an ad-free experience.

**How It Works:**

1. Set your location to get local pollen forecasts
2. Check the daily pollen levels before going outside
3. Log any symptoms you experience throughout the day
4. Review your trends to identify patterns and triggers
5. Get alerts when pollen is high

Whether you're dealing with hay fever, seasonal allergies, or pollen sensitivities, Pollen Tracker helps you stay informed and prepared.

## 3. Privacy Policy

**POLLEN TRACKER PRIVACY POLICY**

**Last Updated: March 2026**

**1. Data We Collect**

Pollen Tracker collects minimal personal data to provide its services:

- **Location Data**: We use your location to provide local pollen forecasts. Location is only used while the app is open and is not stored on our servers.
- **Symptom Logs**: Any symptom data you voluntarily log is stored locally on your device. This data is not shared with third parties.
- **Usage Data**: We collect anonymous, aggregated usage data to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is collected.

**2. How We Use Data**

- Provide accurate, location-based pollen forecasts
- Help you track and understand your allergy symptoms
- Improve our services and user experience
- Send high pollen alerts (if enabled)

**3. Data Storage**

All symptom logs are stored locally on your device using secure storage. You can delete this data at any time through the app settings.

**4. Third-Party Services**

Our app uses the following third-party services:

- **Open-Meteo API**: Provides pollen and air quality data. Your location is sent to this service to retrieve local forecasts.
- **Apple (StoreKit)**: Handles subscription payments securely. We do not access or store your payment information.

**5. Health Data**

Pollen Tracker does not collect HealthKit data or any sensitive health information as defined by Apple. Symptom logging is voluntary and stored only on your device.

**6. Children's Privacy**

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

**7. Changes to This Policy**

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app.

**8. Contact Us**

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer.
